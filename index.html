An interactive guide to the best Electronic Music Festivals in America!!

Welcome to my website, it collects information on handfull of the best music festivals in the country in one easy to use photo album!

East Coast

The east coast has always held its own it the festival scene, from big city NYC events to small town farm gatherings, the party never stops! With events such as Ultra in Miami, Camp Bisco in Pennsylvania, Moonrise in Baltimore, Minus Zero in Vermont, and countless others!

Midwest

With a more dispearsed population, the midwest is home to dozens of great campout events, the two most well known being Electric Forest in Michigan, and Summer Camp in Illinois. Also home to one of America's biggest music festivals, Lollapolozza in Chicago!
